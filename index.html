Is the diagnostic really free?

Yes — completely free. No credit card, no sales call, no obligation. The diagnostic is our way of showing you the quality of our thinking. We hope you'll upgrade to a full audit after you see the value, but there's no pressure either way.

How quickly will I receive it?

Typically within 2–5 minutes of submitting the form. Your diagnostic is generated in real time and emailed directly as a PDF attachment.

What's the difference between the free diagnostic and the paid audit?

The free diagnostic identifies your top opportunities and estimates the value at stake — the what. The paid audit adds the how: complete implementation roadmaps, step-by-step plans, ROI models, vendor shortlists, and a prioritised action plan. The paid audit is what you bring to your team to actually execute.

How accurate are the estimates?

Our AI engine is trained on deployment experience across hundreds of SMEs and mid-market businesses. Opportunity names and value estimates are grounded in real implementation data. They should be treated as informed estimates — the full paid audit includes deeper modelling with more precise ROI calculations.

Do I need technical knowledge to use the report?

No. All reports are written for business owners and senior leaders — not developers. We focus on business outcomes, commercial logic, and practical steps you can act on whether or not you have a tech team.

What happens to my data?

Your intake information is used solely to generate your diagnostic. We do not sell it, share it with third parties, or use it for any other purpose.
